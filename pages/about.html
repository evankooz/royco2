Who We Are

Built on Trust. Proven Over Time.

"A partner you can count on — every order, every time."

Royco Distributors was founded in 1993 with a straightforward mission: bring the best industrial lubricants and supplies to the businesses of the Northeast — and back them with people who actually know the products inside and out.

Over more than thirty years, we've grown from a small regional distributor into one of the Northeast's most respected names in industrial supply. Our success hasn't come from cutting corners or chasing volume — it's come from building real, lasting relationships with our customers and holding ourselves to a higher standard on every order.

We serve manufacturers, fleet operators, automotive shops, construction companies, and industrial facilities of all sizes. Whether you need a single drum or a full-facility supply program, our team will find the right solution and stand behind it completely.
